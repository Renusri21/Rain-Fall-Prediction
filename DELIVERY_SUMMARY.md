## 🔍 WHAT THE MODEL DOES

**Inputs:** Weather data  
- Temperature (°C)
- Humidity (%)
- Air Pressure (hPa)
- Wind Speed (km/h)

**Output:** Rainfall prediction  
- "Yes" - Rainfall expected
- "No" - No rainfall expected
- Confidence percentage

**Example:**
```
Input: 28°C, 70% humidity, 1012 hPa, 9 km/h wind
Output: "Yes, rainfall expected (75% confidence)"
```
